What is Southwest PTFS?

Southwest Airlines PTFS is a fan-made recreation of the real-world Southwest Airlines, built within ROBLOX’s Pilot Training Flight Simulator. Our experience is designed to mirror the journey from your first step into the airport to your final arrival at your destination — with attention to detail at every stage.

What is PTFS?

PTFS, or Pilot Training Flight Simulator, is a flight simulator created by Orange12345 on the ROBLOX platform. Launched in 2010, it remains a massive success with over 91 aircraft and counting. This flight sim is a ROBLOX classic and the biggest of its kind on the platform.
