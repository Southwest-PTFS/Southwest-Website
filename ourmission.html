To Provide

Our mission at Southwest Airlines PTFS is to provide friendly, affordable, reliable, and realistic recreations of Southwest Airlines' daily operations—brought to life in ROBLOX.

To Come Together

No matter where you are in the world, through the power of ROBLOX and Discord, we unite as one community—coming together for more than just realistic flights.

To Learn

No matter who you are, You can go from zero to hero on our staff team with plenty of training documents, resources and our upcoming Southwest Pilot Academy that teaches you everything about aviation and advanced theory
