Rules & Regulations

Keeping and maintaining a safe and LUVING community.

  1. You must abide by Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  2. You must be 13 years of age or older to be in this community.
  3. You must maintain respect for all members regardless of rank, version, or position and keep things positive.
  4. Refrain from using hate speech, discriminatory, or derogatory language.
  5. Refrain from referencing real-life tragedies or making jokes about such tragedies.
  6. Refrain from referencing, describing, or conversing about Sexual or NSFW Topics.
  7. Refrain from advertising your Discord Server, YouTube Channel, or any Social Media in undesignated advertisement channels.
  8. Do not share Personal Information about yourself, another community member, or another PTC member, regardless of their consent. This means photos, names, addresses, or possible Personally Identifying Information.
  9. Refrain from trolling in the server, onboard flights, and being rowdy and annoying in general. We don’t take trolling lightly here.
  10. Moderators may add rules on the fly or moderate users for unlisted rules and choose punishments that fit the action.
  11. Use common sense.
